Speak Up


- What?

Speak Up is an app for girls and women where they can look at each Party in Parliments Policies and get a better understaning of what they are. It is a platform where women can also see where others are using the app and creates the oppourtunity for them to chat and potentially meet in person to discuss their opinions and views on the matters at hand. Speak Up creates a place for girls and women to learn, discuss, and group together to take action against issues that effect them.

- Why?

The inspiration for Speak Up was to create a modern version of Womens sufferage. A way for women who are all fighting for the same change to happen to come together on a social platform instead of going door to door to gain support.

- Who?

Speak Up was created for women and girls to use whether they are involoved, interested, or know nothing about politics within New Zealand and want to make their voices heard.







Features

- Map

You can see where others are using the app whether that be near or far away from you.


- Chat

You can open a chat window with those using the app by clicking on makers on the map.


- Favourite

You can favourite the policies you are most interested in and go straight to it through the
navigation bar
